    Setting up a VPN?

    Setting up a VPN is easy. Follow these steps to enhance your online security

    1. Choose a VPN provider and sign up for an account.
    2. Download and install the VPN application on your device.
    3. Launch the application and log in with your credentials.
    4. Select a server location and connect to the VPN.
